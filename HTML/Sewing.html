Sewing Course

Overview

The Sewing Course is designed to equip participants with the essential skills needed for garment alteration and tailoring. This course covers various aspects of sewing, including techniques for creating new garments and modifying existing ones. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to enhance your sewing skills, this course will empower you to express your creativity through fabric.

Through hands-on practice and expert instruction, participants will learn how to operate sewing machines, work with patterns, and execute various sewing techniques. This course emphasizes practical skills that can be applied to personal projects or professional endeavors in fashion and textile industries.

Course Details

Fees: R1500

Duration: 6 Months

Who Should Attend: This course is ideal for beginners wanting to learn sewing basics, hobbyists looking to expand their skills, and anyone interested in tailoring and fashion design.

Course Content:

  • Types of stitches and their applications.
  • Threading a sewing machine and basic machine maintenance.
  • Sewing buttons, zips, hems, and seams.
  • Techniques for making alterations to existing garments.
  • Designing and sewing new garments from scratch.

Course Outcomes:

  • Ability to operate a sewing machine confidently.
  • Understanding of various sewing techniques and when to apply them.
  • Skills to design and create custom garments.
  • Knowledge to make alterations effectively.
