First Aid Course

Overview

The First Aid Course is designed to provide participants with essential knowledge and skills to respond effectively in emergency situations. This course covers critical topics such as assessing the scene, administering basic life support, and managing various medical emergencies. Whether you are preparing for a career in healthcare or simply wish to be equipped with lifesaving skills, this course will empower you to act confidently in critical situations.

Through a combination of theoretical learning and practical exercises, participants will engage in hands-on training that prepares them to handle emergencies competently. The course emphasizes the importance of quick decision-making and effective communication in emergency scenarios.

Course Details

Fees: R1500

Duration: 6 Months

Who Should Attend: This course is ideal for individuals interested in gaining first aid skills, parents who want to ensure their children's safety, and professionals looking to enhance their workplace safety training.

Course Content:

  • Wounds and bleeding management.
  • Burns and fractures care.
  • Emergency scene management techniques.
  • Cardio-Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR) training.
  • Handling respiratory distress situations, including choking.

Course Outcomes:

  • Ability to assess and respond effectively in emergency situations.
  • Knowledge of first aid techniques and when to apply them.
  • Confidence in providing life-saving interventions.
  • Preparedness to handle various medical emergencies.
