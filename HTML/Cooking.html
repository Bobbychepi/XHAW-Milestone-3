Child Minding Course

Overview

The Child Minding Course is designed to equip participants with the essential skills and knowledge required for providing safe and nurturing care for children. This comprehensive course covers a range of important topics, from understanding child development to implementing effective safety measures in childcare settings.

Whether you aspire to become a professional child minder or simply wish to enhance your childcare abilities, this course will prepare you to create a supportive and engaging environment for children. Our curriculum includes practical skills and theoretical knowledge, ensuring you are well-prepared to meet the unique challenges of caring for children.

Participants will engage in interactive sessions, group discussions, and hands-on activities that foster learning and application of concepts in real-life scenarios.

Course Details

Fees: R750

Duration: 6 Weeks

Who Should Attend: This course is ideal for individuals looking to start a career in childcare, parents wanting to enhance their caregiving skills, and anyone interested in learning about child development and effective childcare practices.

Course Content:

  • Understanding the needs of children from birth to six years.
  • Recognizing developmental milestones in children and how to support them.
  • Creating educational activities tailored for different age groups.
  • Establishing safety and hygiene protocols in childcare settings.
  • Effective communication strategies with children and parents.
  • Basic first aid and emergency response in childcare situations.

Course Outcomes:

  • Ability to provide compassionate and age-appropriate care.
  • Understanding of child behavior and effective management strategies.
  • Skills to create a safe, stimulating, and educational environment for children.
  • Confidence in responding to emergencies and implementing first aid.

Cooking Course Overview

Welcome to our Cooking Course! This program is designed for individuals who wish to enhance their culinary skills while promoting healthy eating habits within their families. Whether you are a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced cook aiming to refine your techniques, this course has something for everyone.

Throughout the course, participants will engage in a variety of hands-on cooking sessions, learn about meal planning, and understand the nutritional needs for different age groups. Our expert instructors will guide you every step of the way, ensuring you leave with the confidence to prepare delicious and nutritious meals.

Course Details

Cooking

Fees: R750

Purpose: This course aims to equip participants with essential cooking skills to prepare and cook nutritious family meals, promoting healthy eating habits within the home.

Course Overview: The Cooking course is designed for individuals of all skill levels, from beginners who have never cooked before to those looking to refine their culinary techniques. Participants will learn the fundamentals of meal planning, ingredient selection, and cooking methods to create delicious and healthy dishes.

Content:

  • Meal Planning: Understand how to plan meals for a week, including budgeting and using seasonal ingredients.
  • Preparation and Cooking Techniques: Learn various cooking methods such as boiling, baking, grilling, and saut√©ing.
  • Understanding Ingredients: Gain knowledge about different types of proteins, carbohydrates, and vegetables, including how to choose and prepare them.
  • Nutritional Requirements: Explore the nutritional needs for different age groups, focusing on the healthy diet required for infants, children, and adults.
  • Kitchen Safety: Emphasize the importance of safety in the kitchen, including proper handling of knives, food storage, and hygiene practices.
  • Hands-On Cooking Sessions: Participate in practical cooking sessions where you will prepare various meals under the guidance of experienced instructors.

Outcome: Upon completion of this course, participants will be able to confidently prepare a variety of nutritious meals for their families, understanding the importance of balanced diets and healthy cooking practices.
