Landscaping Course

Overview

The Landscaping Course is designed to equip participants with the essential skills and knowledge to create and maintain beautiful gardens. This course covers a variety of landscaping techniques, including plant selection, garden design, and the installation of fixed structures. Whether you are a homeowner looking to enhance your outdoor space or an aspiring landscaper, this course will provide you with the tools to transform any garden.

Participants will learn to balance aesthetics and functionality in garden design, understanding the role of both indigenous and exotic plants in creating diverse and sustainable landscapes. The course emphasizes practical skills that can be applied to personal or professional landscaping projects.

Course Details

Fees: R1500

Duration: 6 Months

Who Should Attend: This course is ideal for individuals interested in gardening, homeowners wishing to enhance their gardens, and those seeking a career in landscaping and garden design.

Course Content:

  • Understanding indigenous and exotic plants and trees.
  • Design and installation of fixed structures such as fountains, statues, and benches.
  • Techniques for balancing plants and trees in a garden layout.
  • Aesthetics of plant shapes and colors in landscaping.
  • Effective garden layout design principles.

Course Outcomes:

  • Ability to design and create aesthetically pleasing gardens.
  • Knowledge of plant selection and garden maintenance techniques.
  • Skills in installing and incorporating fixed structures into garden designs.
  • Understanding of how to balance functionality and beauty in landscaping.
