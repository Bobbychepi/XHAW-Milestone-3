Life Skills Course

Overview

The Life Skills Course is designed to equip participants with essential skills to navigate everyday life effectively. This course covers a range of practical topics that empower individuals to manage their personal affairs confidently and competently. Whether you are a young adult preparing for independence or someone looking to enhance your foundational skills, this course will provide you with valuable tools for everyday living.

Participants will learn important skills that are crucial for personal and professional success, fostering independence and self-reliance. The course emphasizes hands-on learning and real-world applications to ensure that participants are well-prepared to face life's challenges.

Course Details

Fees: R1500

Duration: 6 Months

Who Should Attend: This course is ideal for individuals seeking to improve their life skills, young adults entering the workforce, and anyone interested in enhancing their ability to manage personal affairs effectively.

Course Content:

  • How to open and manage a bank account.
  • Understanding basic labor laws and knowing your rights.
  • Developing basic reading and writing literacy skills.
  • Improving basic numeric literacy skills for everyday transactions.

Course Outcomes:

  • Ability to manage personal finances effectively.
  • Understanding of employment rights and labor laws.
  • Improved literacy skills for effective communication.
  • Basic numeracy skills for handling everyday calculations and budgeting.
