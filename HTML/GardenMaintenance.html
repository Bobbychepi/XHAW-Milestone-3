Garden Maintenance Course

Overview

The Garden Maintenance Course provides participants with essential skills and knowledge to cultivate and maintain a healthy and vibrant garden. Whether you are a novice gardener or looking to refine your skills, this course will help you understand the fundamentals of garden care.

The curriculum covers various aspects of garden maintenance, including effective watering techniques, pruning strategies, and planting methodologies. With a hands-on approach, participants will learn how to create aesthetically pleasing garden layouts and care for both indigenous and exotic plants.

Course Details

Fees: R750

Duration: 6 Weeks

Who Should Attend: This course is perfect for gardening enthusiasts, homeowners, and anyone interested in developing their gardening skills for personal or professional use.

Course Content:

  • Understanding water restrictions and the watering needs of indigenous and exotic plants.
  • Learning techniques for pruning and propagating various plant species.
  • Balancing plants and trees for optimal garden health and aesthetics.
  • Planting techniques tailored for different types of plants.
  • Designing and implementing effective garden layouts.

Course Outcomes:

  • Ability to identify the watering requirements of different plant species.
  • Skills to prune and propagate plants successfully.
  • Knowledge of effective garden layout design principles.
  • Confidence in maintaining a healthy and visually appealing garden.
